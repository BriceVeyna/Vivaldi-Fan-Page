Bio

Antonio Vivaldi (1678-1741) was an Italian composer, violinist, teacher, and impresario during the Baroque era of music. He was born in the City-State of Venice, a maritime and cultural hub of Europe. After a brief time as an ordained priest, he found his way to his true calling in music.
